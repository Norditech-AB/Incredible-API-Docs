Our goal is to make it easy to build agentic applications. Behind everything we do is our research into **Agentic Models** — AI systems designed for one metric: *getting real work done*.

We've developed a family of agentic models — from our latest **Agent 4** to specialized models like **SWE-LLM** for Swedish. These models power our API behind the scenes, automatically selected to best handle your request. [Explore our models →](/models)
