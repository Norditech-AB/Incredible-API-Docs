## What This Covers

This page explains how to find agent ideas from other users and how to share your own agents with the community.

## Finding Agents

The Community page shows agents that other Incredible users have shared. Use it to discover ideas or find ready-made solutions.

**Where:** Click **Community** in the sidebar navigation, or go directly to [incredible.one/community](https://incredible.one/community).

### Browsing Options

| Filter | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **Trending** | Popular agents based on recent activity |
| **New** | Recently published agents |
| **Most Upvoted** | Highest-rated agents of all time |
| **Categories** | Filter by type: Sales, Marketing, Support, Research, etc. |

## Sharing Your Agent

Share agents you've built so others can learn from or use them.

### How to Submit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your agent">
    Go to the agent you want to share in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Submit to Community">
    Find the **Share** button (ðŸ“¤) in the topbar, or look for "Submit to Community" in the settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add details">
    Fill in the submission form:
    - **Title:** Clear name describing what it does
    - **Description:** What problem it solves, how it works
    - **Categories:** Select relevant tags (Sales, Marketing, Research, etc.)
    - **Icon:** Choose an icon that represents your agent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Your agent appears in the Community feed for others to discover.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Makes a Good Submission

| Element | Good example | Why |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Title** | "Daily Competitor News Digest" | Clear, describes the outcome |
| **Description** | "Searches for news about specified competitors and sends a morning email summary" | Explains what it does and why it's useful |
| **Categories** | Research, Marketing | Helps people find it |
| **Working agent** | Tested and functional | Others will actually use it |

<Warning>
Test your agent before submitting. Broken agents frustrate users and get downvoted.
</Warning>

## Community Features

### Upvoting

Click the upvote button on agents you find helpful. This:
- Helps good agents surface to more users
- Shows appreciation to the creator
- Builds the creator's reputation

### Top Creators

The leaderboard shows users with the most upvotes across their submissions. Build reputation by sharing useful agents.

### My Submissions

Track your published agents:
- See upvote counts
- Edit or update descriptions
- Remove submissions if needed

Find this in the Community page under **My Submissions**.

### Sharing Links

Each agent has a unique link you can share:
- Copy link from any agent card
- Share directly to LinkedIn, Twitter, or elsewhere
- Send to colleagues who might find it useful
