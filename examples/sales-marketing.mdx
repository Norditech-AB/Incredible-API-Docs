# Sales & Marketing Examples

Discover how to build AI agents for sales automation, lead management, and marketing optimization.

## Lead Management

### CRM Health Checker

**Agent**: Weekly CRM Health Checker @MKAct

**Schedule**: Every Monday at 08:00

**Description**: Find deals that have not been updated in 2 weeks in Hubspot and send recommendations for next actions in Slack.

```bash
curl -X POST "https://api.incredible.one/v1/agents" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{
    "name": "Weekly CRM Health Checker",
    "description": "Find deals that have not been updated in 2 weeks in Hubspot and send recommendations for next actions in Slack",
    "schedule": "every Monday at 08:00",
    "user_id": "your_user_id",
    "integrations": ["hubspot", "slack"],
    "prompt": "Scan Hubspot for deals not updated in 2 weeks, analyze each deal stage, generate personalized next action recommendations, and send a detailed report to the sales team via Slack"
  }'
```

## Analytics & Reporting

### Blog Traffic Reporter

**Agent**: Blog Traffic Reporting @SEO360

**Schedule**: Every 29th at 08:00

**Description**: Fetch all pages with prefix /blog from Google Analytics. Write out a comprehensive monthly summary and add it to the database "Monthly Web Report" in Notion. Make sure to input the date and number of articles in each entry.

```bash
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{
    "name": "Blog Traffic Reporter",
    "description": "Fetch all pages with prefix /blog from Google Analytics. Write out a comprehensive monthly summary and add it to the database 'Monthly Web Report' in Notion. Make sure to input the date and number of articles in each entry",
    "schedule": "every 29th at 08:00",
    "user_id": "your_user_id",
    "integrations": ["google_analytics", "notion"],
    "prompt": "Pull blog traffic data from Google Analytics, analyze performance metrics, create comprehensive monthly report with article counts and dates, and store in Notion database for tracking"
  }'
```
